Best performing student award for Jennifer Simonjan

We are happy to announce that Jennifer Simonjan – a student researcher of the EPiCS team at Alpen-Adria-Universität (AAU) Klagenfurt – received the Best Performing Student Award of the Faculty of Technical Sciences at AAU. This award is handed over to the faculty’s top performing students of the year. Jennifer also completed her master study with distinction three weeks ago.

Let’s congratulate Jennifer for this great success!
