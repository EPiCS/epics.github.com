EPiCS Video Student Contest

Everything you need to know about self-awareness in technical systems
Win up to €500 in cash

Compose a short video explaining self-awareness:

  • Focus on methods for self-organization of technical systems; explain the benefits and shortcomings
  • Make your video self-contained and narrate it so that it is accessible to a broad online audience.
  • Generate a (maximum) 2 minutes video with English narration or English subtitles
  • If applicable, talk about your own research work

Submission:

  • Make your video available for download on a (preferably password protected) ftp or web site
  • Follow the video compression guidelines to create your videos.
  • Send the link and password to your video to contest@epics-project.eu

Evaluation criteria:

  • Relevance
  • Entertainment value
  • Presentation

Awards:

  • €500, €300 and €200 cash prizes for the top three videos
  • Accepted videos will be promoted via the EPiCS video channel on vimeo. Authors of accepted videos will be required to sign a distribution license form.

Important Dates:

  • Submission deadline: July 31, 2013
  • Notification: August 31, 2013
